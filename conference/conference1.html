The International Conference on Emerging Trends in English Language Teaching ICETELT'19 will be held in India on March 8,9 2019 conducted by Department of English,School of Science and Humanities,Kongu Engineering College,Erode,Tamilnadu.

This is a large scale academic conference envisaged as one of the biggest academic international conferences on English Language Teaching in India.

This Conference seeks to promote the development of English Language Teaching[ELT] and research in India and pave the way for enhanced Communication between ELT practitioners and their counterparts in other countries.

Venue: Kongu Engineering College,Perundurai,Erode.
Conference: 8-9 March 2019
